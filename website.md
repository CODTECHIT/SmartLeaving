Project Title

Premium Corporate Services Website

Objective

Build a high-end, premium-looking corporate website for a multi-service business offering:

Industrial Services
Real Estate Services
Insurance Services

The website must look minimal, elegant, and corporate, not template-based or AI-generated.

Design Guidelines
Style: Premium, modern corporate
Color palette:
Primary: Deep Navy / Charcoal Black
Secondary: Gold / Subtle gradient accents
Background: Clean white or soft grey
Typography:
Headings: Bold, modern sans-serif
Body: Clean, readable font
Layout:
Spacious (good padding/margins)
Grid-based
Smooth animations (not flashy)
Avoid:
Overused templates
Cartoonish icons
Bright or neon colors
“AI-looking” sections
Pages & Structure
1. Home Page
Hero Section
Strong headline (corporate tone)
Subtext about trust, scale, and expertise
CTA button (e.g., Explore Services)
Services Overview (3 Sections)

Display 3 main categories:

Industrial Services
Real Estate Services
Insurance

Each should have:

Icon or minimal graphic
Short description
“Learn More” button
2. Industrial Services Page
Section: Industrial Properties

List the following projects:

Project 1 – Active Pharmaceutical Company
Project 2 – Active Pharmaceutical Company
Project 3 – Pharmaceutical Formulation Company

Functionality:

Clicking a project should open a PDF/PPT viewer or download file
Section: Funding Services

Private Funding to Industries

Description:
“We provide funding up to ₹500 Crores without collateral (Terms & Conditions apply)”

Equity Funding to Industries

Short professional description
3. Real Estate Services Page

List categories clearly with premium layout:

Farmlands
Commercial Lands
Open Plots
Villa Plots
Apartment Flats
New Flats
Resale Flats

Each category:

Clean card layout
Optional image background
Clickable for future expansion
4. Insurance Services Page

Categories:

Group Health Insurance
Property Insurance
Personal Insurance (Health Insurance)
Life Insurance

Each section:

Short description
Clean icon or visual
Professional tone
Navigation

Top Navbar:

Home
Industrial Services
Real Estate
Insurance
Contact

Sticky navigation preferred

Footer

Include:

Company overview (short)
Quick links
Contact details
Minimal social icons
Functional Requirements
Responsive (mobile + desktop)
Smooth scrolling
Fast loading
PDF/PPT viewer integration for projects
Clean hover effects
Tone & Content Style
Corporate
Trust-focused
No exaggerated marketing language
Use phrases like:
“Trusted Solutions”
“Strategic Investments”
“Reliable Partnerships”
Special Instructions
Avoid generic AI layouts
Make it look like a custom-designed corporate website
Focus on structure, whitespace, and typography
Keep it premium, simple, and powerful
